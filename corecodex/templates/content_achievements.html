» About Achievements

What are Achievements?

Achievements are rewards for completing certain tasks, goals, or cool little tricks found on CoreCodex. There are a dozen achievements given out for completing certain numbers, difficulty, and specific programming challenges, as well as many others that are here to entice you to challenge yourself and chellenge us!

How do I get Achievements?

Each achievement is listed on your user page and also give you user points! Show them off by linking your account page corecodex.com/users/[your user name] to your friends or just bask in your own awesome glory, racking in all those extra user points. Either way, have fun!
