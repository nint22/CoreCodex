Want to use CoreCodex for your class, university, or school?

CoreCodex is currently a free educational tool. If you would like to manage your grading or automate code testing of your student's work, simply register your account and click on your user name to generate new programming challenges and manage your course roster.

Please note that CoreCodex and CoreS2 reserves the right to require an educational license fee in the future.
